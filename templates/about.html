About Us

ShareToYou was founded in 2020. It is a website that provides a platform for users to rent different commodities. Our goal: sustainable life: reduce the chance of items being discarded by renting between different users, and borrowers can use idle items to earn income. The tenant can also save money.

Contact Us by Email: sharetoyouhk@gmail.com

How to post your item?

First, you need to register your account.

After registered, you can login to our user Dashboard page.

Inside Dashboard page, you will see a "Add Item" button, press it and you can add you items to our website and rent it out!

Enjoy!
